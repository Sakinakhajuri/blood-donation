About us



What is the use of this website?

     The main aim to develop this website was to donate blood to the ones in need. As we know blood donation is required for various purposes. In the current situation when we require blood we need to contact the blood banks and acquire the blood. But the specific blood group may not be available at times. Thus,there is an urge to provide a system wherein we can track a donor and save a life of our loved ones. Hence,this webite is developed to eradicate the faults in existing system.


                    
                    

How does the website operate?

     Firstly,we have 5 links in our navigation bar i.e. Home,Register as Donor,Search for Donor,About us and Contact us.
1. Home-This link redirects you to the main page.
2. Register As Donor- If you wish to donate blood and if you're above 18 years you can register here with your credentials.
3. Search As Donor- If you're searching for a donor you can specify the details of patient in need and you'll get a list of people with the correct match along with the contact details. Thus you can reach out to them.
4. About Us- This link will redirect you to the current page.
5. Contact Us- This link will redirect you to a page where we've specified our contact details. You can also reach out to us by typing in your message in the given textboxes along with the credentials.


This website is purely developed for a social cause. We hope that we end up saving a life of your loved one!!
